人物塑造篇

Character Creation

试着用柔和的笔触去捕捉青春的灵动，蓬松且自然卷曲的发丝，不需要多么规整束缚，让每根线条都透露着鲜活的灵气。眉眼间，保留些许的清透与锐利，是年轻女孩特有的朝气与倔强。让她从画布上，悄悄地走到你眼前……

Try to capture the vitality of youth with soft brushstrokes, fluffy and naturally curly hair, no need for too much regularity and restraint, let every line reveal vivid灵气. Between the eyebrows and eyes, retain a little clarity and sharpness, which is the unique vitality and stubbornness of young girls. Let her quietly walk before your eyes from the canvas...

人像色彩篇

Portrait Color

色彩与我而言并不是 简单的填充，而像是情绪 的呼吸。 暖调是亲昵的絮语， 唇上的红、眼里的光，都 是情绪的标点，让每一张 面孔的故事都有了更加鲜 活的停顿与转折。 这些斑斓的色彩最终 落向的从来不是眼睛，而 是我们的内心……

For me, color is not simply filling, but like the breath of emotion. Warm tones are intimate whispers, the red on the lips, the light in the eyes, all are punctuation of emotion, giving each face's story more vivid pauses and turns. These colorful hues never ultimately fall on the eyes, but on our hearts...

场景概念篇

Scene Concept

在场景概念设计中，我尝试构建富有故事性的空间环境，通过光影、构图和细节的处理，创造出具有沉浸感的视觉体验。每一个场景都承载着独特的叙事性，让观者能够感受到画面背后的故事和情感。

In scene concept design, I try to build narrative spatial environments, creating immersive visual experiences through the handling of light, shadow, composition and details. Each scene carries a unique narrative, allowing viewers to feel the story and emotion behind the image.

分镜设计篇

Storyboard Design

在分镜设计中，我尝试通过连续的画面叙事，构建完整的故事流程。通过镜头角度、构图和节奏的把握，创造出富有动感和情感张力的视觉叙事，让故事能够生动地呈现在观众面前。

In storyboard design, I try to build a complete story flow through continuous visual narrative. Through the mastery of camera angles, composition and rhythm, I create visual narratives with dynamic and emotional tension, allowing the story to be vividly presented to the audience.

个人书法练习

Personal Calligraphy Practice

在书法练习中，我追求传统与现代的融合，通过笔墨的运用，表达对文字艺术的理解与感悟。每一笔每一划都承载着对传统文化的尊重与传承，同时融入个人的艺术风格与情感表达。

In calligraphy practice, I pursue the integration of tradition and modernity, expressing my understanding and perception of the art of writing through the use of brush and ink. Each stroke carries respect and inheritance of traditional culture, while incorporating personal artistic style and emotional expression.
